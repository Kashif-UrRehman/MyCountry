Karhai

karahi is a type of thick, circular, and deep cooking pot that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is used in Pakistani, Indian, Afghani, Bangladeshi, and Nepalese cuisines. Traditionally press-formed from mild steel sheet or made of wrought iron, karahi look like woks with steeper sides.
